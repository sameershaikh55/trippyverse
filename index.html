Roadmap

Curious for how we as a team see this project going? Check out the roadmap below to feel just as excited as we are to make this project happen!

Stage 1 - 0%

Early supporters will gain access to both a number of giveaways and the whitelist for the TRIPPY presale.

Stage 2 - 15%

Those who are on the whitelist will get the chance to purchase TRIPPYs from our 1111 stock private presale, significantly raising your odds of being able to acquire one of our unique buddies.

Stage 3 - 25%

Once the presale has sold out, $25,000 will be donated to a community-chosen charity foundation.

Stage 4 - 40%

After the presale but before the public release, 7 TRIPPYs will be given away via social media.

Stage 5 - 50%

Following this, 7760 TRIPPYs will be released onto the Solana blockchain, and become publicly available resulting in the existence of 8888 TRIPPYs.

Stage 6 - 60%

Once all TRIPPYs have been sold, another $25,000 will be donated to a second community-chosen charity foundation.

Stage 7 - 70%

TRIPPYs will be listed on both Solanart and Digital Eyes marketplaces, allowing everyone, even those who missed out on our release, to join in on the fun.

Stage 8 - 80%

TRIPPYs are at the forefront of innovation, and are effectively the Tesla of NFTs. For this reason, the chance to win one of three Tesla Model 3's, for free, will be available to all TRIPPY holders.
